s - settlement

Urbanize Faiyum by building a new settlement.

Place 1 settlement on any crocodile-free resource space of your choice. That space is now developed. Any worker present remains on this space. A gap of at least 1 space must be present between two settlements, but settlements on opposite spaces of the channel are okay since the channel separates these spaces. Pay 1 wheat, 1 grape, and 1 stone. Gain 3 reputation and $3. You can gain 1 extra reputation as explained under S - TWO ROADS.

Example:: Hedwig plays S - SETTLEMENT and places a settlement on the crocodile-free wheat space 1. . She pays 1 wheat, 1 grape and 1 stone, then gains 3 reputation and $3. The next settlement could be placed on the spaces as they are the necessary distance from the existing settlements. The space is not crocodile-free, so Hedwig could not place a settlement there. adjacent to the new settlement cannot be chosen, even though it is crocodile-free.
