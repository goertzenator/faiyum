62 - novices

Some chosen workers aspire to a clerical life.

Remove 1-3 upright workers from any spaces. You cannot choose prone workers from 60 - GEM MINERS.

Gain $2 per removed worker.

Example: Hedwig plays 60 - GEM MINERS. She uses the first option and lays down 3 workers by paying $3 1. . These workers will remain on these spaces until Hedwig plays GEM MINERS again and chooses to remove all prone workers.

Next, Nicole plays 62 - NOVICES. She cannot choose the prone workers and instead removes 3 standing workers of her choice 2. , then gains $6.
