54 - expansion

To cope with demand, increase the size of the workshops.

Place 1 expansion next to any workshop without an expansion. Any worker present remains on this space. Regarding other card actions, this space is still considered to be a workshop.

Pay 1 resource of your choice (grape, stone, wheat, or fish). Gain 3 reputation.

Example: Nicole plays 54 - EXPANSION. She places the expansion on the grape workshop 1. , pays 1 stone, then gains 3 reputation.

Next, Angelika plays 56 - ASSISTANT. She places a worker on the wheat workshop 2. and gains 3 wheat. She could have chosen the grape workshop to gain 3 grapes instead.
