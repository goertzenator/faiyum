88 - papyrus

A good scribe can give you access to many abilities.

Copy the action of the top card on your discard stack.

Example: Angelika plays 88 - PAPYRUS. The last card she played was 64 - BAZAAR (see above), so Angelika chooses the card 76 - ESTATE 1. , places that card in her hand, then pays $2. Then, she draws 1 card from the draw pile and places it into the card market.
