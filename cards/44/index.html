44 - farm

Hungry people demand food.

Place 1 workshop on any wheat space that is already developed by a road or bridge. Any worker present remains on this space.

Pay 1 grape and 1 stone. Gain 3 reputation and 3 wheat.

Example: Hedwig plays 44 - FARM. She places 1 workshop on the wheat space developed by the bridge 1. . Hedwig pays 1 grape and 1 stone, then gains 3 reputation and 3 wheat.

If she had played 46 - VINEYARD instead, she could place a workshop on one of the three spaces connected by roads.
